Introducing Sheprd: Your Trusted Scam Detection App

In today's world, scam attempts are becoming more sophisticated. Whether it's a phone call, a text message, or an email, scammers are constantly finding new ways to trick you. That's why we created Sheprd, an app designed to help you identify if a call, message, or email is likely to be a scam.

How Does Sheprd Work?

Sheprd offers three layers of defense to protect against scams. Our goal is to help you stay safe and secure while providing you with the knowledge and tools you need to avoid falling victim to scam attempts.

1. Knowledge

Knowledge is power. We provide a set of educational resources, including a questionnaire, to help you understand the steps you can take to avoid scams. This feature primarily focuses on social engineering attacks, providing you with the information you need to stay calm and make rational decisions.

2. Chat Support

Our AI-powered chat support is there for you during a potential scam attempt. This chat system is designed to help you stay calm and think rationally, offering immediate guidance and support when you need it most.

3. Advanced Threat Detection

Our advanced threat detection software is designed to intercept and assess calls or messages that could pose a threat. This technology aims to intimidate scammers and provide an additional level of security and comfort for our users.
